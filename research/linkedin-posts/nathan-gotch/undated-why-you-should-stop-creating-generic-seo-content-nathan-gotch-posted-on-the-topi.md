## Original Content
Co-founder & CEO at Rankability

STOP creating generic SEO content targeting top-of-the-funnel informational keywords. You will get slaughtered... And you're also wasting your time. The barrier to entry for informational content is huge now. If you're attacking an info keyword, be ready to invest crazy amounts of capital, time, and effort. This isn't a job for a jack-of-all-trades writer. You'll need: ▪️ A true subject matter expert ▪️ An editor who make the content actually engaging ▪️ Unique data ▪️ Expert nuance ▪️ Real stories ▪️ And beautiful, custom design Is the juice worth the squeeze? Maybe. But here's the funny part: Most businesses won't even try because the ROI will be hard to justify. And that's exactly why there's an opportunity. Those that have the expertise and willingness to make big content investments, might win. The takeaway is this: Do what AI can't. Also, please, I beg of you... Don't create another blog post like "how to train your dog."
