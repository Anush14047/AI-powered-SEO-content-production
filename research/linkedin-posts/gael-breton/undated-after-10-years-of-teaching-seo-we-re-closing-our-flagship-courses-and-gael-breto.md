After 10 years of teaching SEO, we're closing our flagship courses. And no, SEO isn't dead. But the content site model is. When Mark Webster and I started Authority Hacker in 2014, building content sites was the perfect business model for new entrepreneurs. ✅ Low barrier to entry. ✅ Predictable growth. ✅ Solid ROI. But in 2024 and going forward? That model is broken for most people. I've been to countless SEO events recently where everyone keeps a straight face and pretends everything is fine. I won't do that (and we've called Google out all year in our news videos). The Google updates, AI content tsunami, and dramatic SERP changes have fundamentally altered the game. And unlike previous updates where we could adapt and overcome, this time is different. So we're doing something that feels scary but right: We're closing TASS and AH Pro, our main courses that generated 8 figures in revenue. Why? Because I refuse to sell courses teaching strategies that no longer work reliably for most people. What's next for Authority Hacker? Honestly? We don't know yet. But we've always been about finding what actually works in digital marketing and sharing it with brutal honesty. That won't change. The podcast will continue. The free content will flow (but expect some changes in direction next year). And when we find the next big opportunity, you'll be the first to know. To everyone who's been part of this journey since 2014: Thank you. The skills you've learned about website building, content creation, and digital marketing are still valuable. They just need to be applied differently now. Sometimes the best way forward is to admit when something's not working anymore and have the courage to try something new. ❤️ to our incredible team that has helped us throughout the journey. Here's to the next chapter. 🚀
