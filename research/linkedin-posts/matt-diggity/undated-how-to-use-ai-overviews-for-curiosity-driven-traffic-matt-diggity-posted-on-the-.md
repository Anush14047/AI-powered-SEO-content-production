Most businesses think informational searches don't convert, but our client's AI traffic tells a different story. The key isn’t chasing definitions that get fully answered in AI Overviews… It’s targeting questions that spark curiosity and drive clicks. ❌ "What is yoga" - Gets fully answered in AI Overview ✅ "Best yoga poses for stress relief" - Creates curiosity gap that drives clicks ❌ "What are business hours" - No click needed ✅ "Best time to visit shopping mall to avoid crowds" - Needs deeper context We found these using Ahrefs Keywords Explorer, focusing on comparison and benefit-focused searches rather than basic definitions. Low search volume but high likelihood of a deeper dive; exactly what makes them powerful. AI Overviews aren't traffic killers. They're discovery engines when you target the right queries.
