Google's AI Overviews are a trap. You either get shown with no clicks, or get clicks but no features. We figured out how to get both: Here’s our two-part strategy: Part 1: Make it extractable • Lead with concise answer in first 1-2 sentences • Use clear subheadings that match the question • Structure with bullet points and numbered lists • Add TL;DR sections for quick scanning Part 2: Create curiosity gaps • Expand beyond what AI Overview covers • Include why, how, and which option is best • Add unique data, examples, or practical tips • Offer actionable insights competitors miss Example: For "best yoga poses for stress relief" • Start with quick list (extractable) • Then explain why each works, when to use them, variations for different skill levels (click-worthy) The result? We got our client featured for 155 keywords in AI overviews with referral traffic increasing by 370%, all in just 8 months. AI search isn't about getting the snippet - it's about making the snippet a teaser for your full content.
