Building autonomous AI businesses · previously founder & bootstrapper at Clearscope

Ranch-style SEO, or the disaggregation of SEO content to build topical authority is sparking lots of engagement in the community. Some say, "duh, we've been doing topic clusters / hub & spoke for a long time" and that's great! But have you actually been approaching your content strategy right? Google is cracking down on "unoriginal" content, which is coming after your traditional topic clusters / hub & spoke strategy. Why? Because Search Generative Experience & Large Language Models are great at producing answers for high "consensus" answers which are your "what, how, best" query types... To stay relevant as a search engine, Google knows that they need to serve Experience-rich, Perspective-led content that's Engaging 🤝 That's the main takeaway I want to impart with Ranch-Style SEO—it's not about just slicing your content into smaller pieces that target "longer-tail keywords". Ranch-Style SEO targets distinct perspectives in search journeys. The future of SEO is about architecting brilliant content strategies ✍
