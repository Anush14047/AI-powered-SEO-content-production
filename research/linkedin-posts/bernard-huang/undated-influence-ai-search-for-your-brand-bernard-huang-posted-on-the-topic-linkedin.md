AI search, GEO, AEO, LLMO... or whatever you want to call it. If you aren't thinking about how to influence AI responses, your brand will become invisible in the new AI-driven search world.

Growth Advisor | AI SEO for B2B SaaS

EMERGING SEO TREND: I am finding that traffic from ChatGPT has a higher website conversion rate than Google, but conversions from Google are driving WAY bigger B2B deal sizes 💰 I have a client (mid-market B2B SaaS) where leads from Google are driving opportunities in the range of $100K - $300K compared to AI leads at ~$5K a pop. That's a 37x difference in average deal size! Google search is attributable to more qualified, enterprise-level buyers ready for substantial investments, while AI leads typically yield smaller, exploratory purchases. But there's more nuance to the story. Zero click conversions are the missing piece, and SEO attribution is cooked due to the dark SEO funnel. It’s harder to measure conversions from ChatGPT because most answers that cause conversions are zero click. The conversion pattern I’m observing for B2B SaaS: AI = top of funnel discovery, exploration, narrowing. Google = Brand / Direct = conversion. Searchers still need to validate what LLMs are telling them. Rand Fishkin said it best. Google just captures the demand after it gets created elsewhere. Google gets "credit" for being the natural last stop in the conversion path. In other words, Google is just collecting promo flyers from hungry people who were already walking into the pizza shop.
