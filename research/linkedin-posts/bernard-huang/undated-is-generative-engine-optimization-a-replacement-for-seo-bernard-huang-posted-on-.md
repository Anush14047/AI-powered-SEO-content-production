I'm seeing a lot of hype around "Generative Engine Optimization." While I'm excited about the potential of optimizing for AI-driven content creation and discovery, I'm also skeptical. Is #GEO truly a replacement for #SEO ? Or is it simply an evolution of it? Here are some questions I'm grappling with: * How do we measure the success of GEO strategies? Traditional metrics like rankings and traffic may not apply. * What ethical considerations do we need to address when optimizing for generative AI? * How can we ensure that GEO strategies are user-centric and provide real value to searchers? I'd love to hear your thoughts. Are you investing in GEO? If so, what strategies are you using?
